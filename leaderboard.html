Leaderboards

BALSA provides leaderboards for systematic evaluation and comparison of explanation methods. Each explainer in BALSA can be thought of as a benchmark. For an explainer to be useful for a specific application, it needs to consistently perform well across multiple evaluation metrics. Hence, we group individual datasets and explainers in BALSA into meaningful groups, which we refer to as evaluation groups.
